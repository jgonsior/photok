Welcome to photok

photok is the best web application to create and take part in photo contests

What can you do?

Users should be able to register and log in. They should be able to create a contest and participate in every contest.
A participation consists of sending a picture for a specific contest.
When the contest ends, a group of specific users (the jury) will decide on winners.
There can also be a "people's prize", where users have to vote for their favourite image.
